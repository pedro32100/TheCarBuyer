Trade In

When trading in that used car for the car of your dreams one thing that everyone should know. The dealer will never give you the book value for your car. This is because they are trying to sell your car for you but there are something’s that you should know in order to get the most out of that trade in.

  • Know the value of the car and what is an acceptable trade in price. There are websites that help you check the value of the car you want to trade in and offer a good estimate as to what you’ll get.
  • Also keep in mind a dealer will give you a lower price if he has several types of your car in inventory.
  • Timing, trading in your car during certain season will fetch you more coin. Convertibles traded in during the summer will fetch more than in the winter because of off season.
  • Appearance, a clean car would help fetch a little more coin because of the overall perception that the owner took good care of the car.
  • Always negotiate your new car price before offering to trade in your car. This would help you get the most out of your trade.
  • Check for tax deductions, there are 8 states that allow the dealer to deduct the price of a trade in from the sale price to help lower the bill on taxes.
